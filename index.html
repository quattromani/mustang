Condition

This pony has been under a car cover in the garage for the last 5 years; brought out a few times to turn the engine over and do a couple of laps around the block.

There is some body damage at the back right quarter panel (see images in gallery below), and there is some minor rust starting at points, but she still looks great on the road.
